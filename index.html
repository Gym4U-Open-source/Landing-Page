What is GYM4U?


Gym4U is a web platform that offers the services of personal trainers, where they will make use of our platform to invite their clientele and, in this way, invited people can follow your training plan.





For Personal Trainers

Personal trainers will be able to better manage their clients, and thus be able to provide them with personalized routines.

For Customers

Clients will be able to contact a personal trainer so that they can follow their training plan and with a qualification system they will be able to give merit to the personal trainer.
